The Southern
Illinois University
Skateboard Society

We are students in the Southern Illinois area who enjoy skateboarding and longboarding.

The SIU skateboarding society was founded four years ago with the goal of building a skatepark in Carbondale, Illinois.

Objective

The old skatepark at SIU was torn down in 2010 because the ramps were starting to fall apart.

Thousands of students, teachers and community members enjoyed this park for over a decade. Without a permanent location to practice we are forced to share a space with the hockey club.

Carbondale would benefit from building a skatepark, attracting new students and tourists from the surrounding area.

About Us

Founded in 2010, we are a group of people pasionate about skateboarding.

We have a long history of helping the community and having a great time.
