Skateboard Society

We are students in the Southern Illinois area who enjoy skateboarding and longboarding.

We were formed four years ago with the goal of building a skatepark in Carbondale Illinois.

Objective

The old skatepark at SIU was torn down years ago because the ramps were wooden and starting to fall apart.

Thousands of students, teachers and community members enjoyed the park for over a decade.

Now students are without a place to practice an increasingly popular sport.
