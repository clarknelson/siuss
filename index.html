Skateboard Society

We are students in the Southern Illinois area who enjoy skateboarding and longboarding.

Our club was founded four years ago with the goal of building a skatepark in Carbondale, Illinois.

Objective

The old skatepark at SIU was torn down in 2010 because the ramps were starting to fall apart.

Thousands of students, teachers and community members enjoyed this park for over a decade. Without a permanent location we are forced to share a space with the hockey club.

Carbondale would benefit from building a skatepark, attracting new students and tourists from the surrounding area.
